Corona Diary (WIP)

Elliot Z. Lin

2020年 3月 18日

Today I lifted weights in the morning. I had mixed feelings because I felt good getting back into my morning gym routine but frustrated because my form was still nagging me. No matter.

Work today was productive, but I felt more resistance than I did yesterday. Maybe the euphoria of working from home is wearing off. I hope I can keep up my focus for the coming weeks. That would be disappointing if I had to admit to myself I couldn't focus at home, despite my best efforts at maintaining a routine and dressing up for work.

After work I DoorDashed for a bit before making a Costco run. I did good. Despite dashing for only 1.25 hours and making only 3 deliveries, I made more money tonight than I did on any of my other nights. Bless you customers who tipped me so generously! I don't deserve it, but you gave me the benefit of the doubt regardless. Other thoughts: traffic was really light on the streets, which was extra nice. It's rare I get to drive around town in the evening twilight in such peace. I think I'll do more DoorDashing in the coming weeks.

I went to Costco to pick up some eggs and salted butter. I came home with a whole cartload of food for the coming weeks. Come on now, I was buying for my brother too! On a similar note to my DoorDash story, it was also incredibly empty at the Costco, emptier than on regular weekdays. What a treat!

Not that my polished work is any good to begin with, but you catch my drift, yeah? When I have the content written out, I will remove this notice. Cheers!
